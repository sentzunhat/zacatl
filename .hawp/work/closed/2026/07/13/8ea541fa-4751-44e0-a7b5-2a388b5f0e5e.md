## Investigation

Confirmed HIGH for node:sqlite, MED for mongoose/sequelize (adversarially
verified).

- `src/service/layers/infrastructure/orm/nodesqlite/adapter.ts:137-159` —
  `findMany` executes a full-table `SELECT` with no WHERE or LIMIT and filters
  in JavaScript, synchronously on the event loop. O(table) memory and an
  event-loop stall per call; the JSON blob data column means no index can help.
- `src/service/layers/infrastructure/orm/mongoose/adapter.ts:91-100` and
  `src/service/layers/infrastructure/orm/sequelize/adapter.ts:68-72` — broad
  filters buffer the entire collection into heap; no pagination surface exists
  anywhere in the infrastructure layer.

## Plan
